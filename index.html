📋 How to Use Clip Queue Player

Adding Clips

Go to the Queue tab → tap + Add Clip. Paste a YouTube URL and enter start/end timestamps in HH:MM:SS format.

Playback & Controls

Tap anywhere on the video to pause/play — this uses YouTube's native controls. The gear icon (⚙) in the bottom-right of the player lets you change quality. The control bar auto-hides after a few seconds of playback.

Controls

  • Prev / Next — jump between clips (tap video during playback)
  • Stop — end playback and reset

Import / Export

Use the Export/Import buttons in the header to save or share your queue as a .json file.

Browser Limitations

  • Fullscreen requires a user gesture — the app requests it on first Start.
  • YouTube's autoplay policy may require user interaction before audio plays.
  • Private/deleted/age-restricted videos will show an error and skip automatically.
  • Android Chrome may exit fullscreen when the screen locks — re-tap Start to resume.
  • The YouTube IFrame API may not honor seek accuracy to within ±1 second due to video segment boundaries.
